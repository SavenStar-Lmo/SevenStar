Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Detail

Melbourne Chauffeur & Limo Service was founded in 2010 with a single car and a simple belief: that every passenger deserves to travel with dignity, comfort and certainty. What began as a one-man operation from Melbourne's inner suburbs has grown into one of Victoria's most respected private transport services.

We are a family-owned business — not a faceless rideshare platform. Every booking is handled by a real person, every driver is personally vetted, and every vehicle is maintained to the highest standard.

Our client base spans corporate executives, interstate visitors, wedding parties, and everyday Melburnians who refuse to compromise on quality.

James Hartley
Founder & Director
