Cancellations & Refunds

We understand that plans change. SevenStar Limo's cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 24 hours' notice: Full refund. No cancellation fee.
  • Between 4 and 24 hours' notice: 50% of the Booking fare is charged as a cancellation fee.
  • Less than 4 hours' notice or no-show: 100% of the Booking fare is charged. No refund will be issued.
  • Special events and peak periods (New Year's Eve, Grand Prix, major concerts): A minimum 72-hour cancellation notice is required for a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours attract a 100% cancellation fee.

Refunds, where applicable, will be processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. To request a cancellation, please contact SevenStar Limo{% if contact %} by phone on {{ contact.phone }} or email at {{ contact.email }}{% endif %} with your Booking reference number.

SevenStar Limo reserves the right to cancel a Booking in circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to extreme weather, traffic incidents, vehicle breakdown or driver illness. In such cases, a full refund will be issued or an alternative vehicle arranged where possible.
05

Passenger Conduct

All Passengers are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and lawful manner during the Service. SevenStar Limo Drivers have the right to terminate a journey without refund if any Passenger:

  • Is abusive, threatening or aggressive towards the Driver or any other person.
  • Is intoxicated to a degree that poses a risk to themselves, the Driver or the Vehicle.
  • Smokes or vapes inside the Vehicle.
  • Tampers with, damages or soils the Vehicle or its fittings.
  • Causes an unreasonable disturbance or engages in unlawful behaviour.

The Client will be held responsible for any damage caused to the Vehicle by Passengers. Cleaning and repair costs will be invoiced at market rates and are payable within 7 days.

Seatbelts must be worn by all Passengers at all times. Eating is not permitted in Vehicles without prior arrangement. Alcohol consumption in Vehicles is subject to applicable Victorian law and may only be permitted in licensed hire vehicles with prior written consent from SevenStar Limo.

06

Wait Time & No-Show

SevenStar Limo Drivers will wait for the following complimentary periods beyond the scheduled pickup time:

  • Standard point-to-point and hourly bookings: 15 minutes complimentary wait time.
  • Domestic Melbourne airport pickups (Tullamarine): 45 minutes complimentary from the scheduled flight arrival time.
  • International Melbourne airport pickups: 60 minutes complimentary from the scheduled flight arrival time.

After the complimentary period expires, wait time is charged at the applicable hourly rate, billed in 15-minute increments. If no contact is made with SevenStar Limo within the wait period, the Driver may depart and the Booking will be treated as a no-show. No-show Bookings are charged at 100% of the fare.

SevenStar Limo monitors all flights in real time. If your inbound flight is delayed, your Driver will be automatically updated and will adjust their arrival accordingly at no additional charge.

Airport Transfers

For all Melbourne airport pickup Bookings, Passengers should meet their SevenStar Limo Driver at the designated meet-and-greet area in the arrivals hall, unless otherwise specified at the time of Booking.

  • Your Driver will carry a name sign for easy identification.
  • Please provide your flight number at the time of Booking to enable real-time flight tracking by SevenStar Limo.
  • Luggage allowance is subject to the capacity of the booked Vehicle. Additional baggage requirements should be declared at the time of Booking.
  • SevenStar Limo services both Melbourne (Tullamarine) Airport and Avalon Airport. Please specify the correct airport at time of Booking.
  • For drop-off transfers, the Client is responsible for allowing sufficient travel time, particularly during Melbourne peak hours. SevenStar Limo is not liable for missed flights due to unforeseen traffic conditions.
